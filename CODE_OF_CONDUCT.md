# Code of Conduct

Our goal is to create an inclusive, welcoming community for everyone who uses or contributes to this project. All participants are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Standards

Be respectful and kind. Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Insults, slurs, or demeaning language directed at others.
- Threats, stalking, or sustained disruption of conversations or events.
- Posting private or personally identifying information without permission.

Be considerate:
- Assume good faith when others make mistakes.
- Give constructive, specific feedback.
- Respect people's time and bandwidth.

Be collaborative:
- Use clear language and provide context where helpful.
- Share credit and attribution for contributions.
- Follow repository contributing guidelines when opening issues or PRs.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issue trackers, pull requests, project discussion, chat channels, mailing lists, social media) and in-person events organized by the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at: [REPLACE-WITH-EMAIL]. Reports should include as much detail as possible (links, screenshots, timestamps).

The maintainers will review and respond in a timely manner. Possible responses include:
- Requesting that the participant stop the behavior.
- Temporary or permanent banning from project spaces.
- Removal of offending content or revocation of contributor privileges.
